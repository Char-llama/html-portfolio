The Best Movies According to Charlotte

My Top Movies from the 2000's

...because we all know this was the best decade



Legally Blonde

Just iconic. Reese Witherspoon is like a Barbie and we love her for this role.

The Parent Trap

Would you believe this is Lindsay Lohan? Can watch this on repeat.

The Princess Diaries

Another iconic film. Love, love, love. Anne Hathaway is amazing.

The Devil Wears Prada

Would love this just for the fashion. But what a cast to boot too.

She's The Man

Bit of a guilty pleasure... Channing Tatum and Amanda Bynes are a hilarious duo.

Step Up

This sets my dance soul on fire. I have played the final dance (and song) on repeat.

What A Girl Wants

Seeing a trend yet? Amanda Bynes at her best. Funny and tugs on the heart strings with the father/daughter relationship.

Miss Congeniality

FBI + pageants + Sandra Bullock.
